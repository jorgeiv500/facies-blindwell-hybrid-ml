## Representative Blind Well

The repository uses `Well_06` as the representative blind-well case.

Relative to `ProbabilisticEnsemble`, `GeoConstrainedEnsemble` in `Well_06`:

- improves accuracy from `0.874` to `0.895`
- improves macro-F1 from `0.599` to `0.641`
- uses a learned smoothing strength of `0.40`

At the same time, not every continuity metric improves in that single fold, which is why the repository reports fold-level trade-offs instead of claiming universal monotonic gains.
